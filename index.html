About the Sun Pharma Science Foundation (SPSF)

The Sun Pharma Science Foundation (SPSF) is dedicated to advancing science and healthcare in India by recognizing excellence in medical and pharmaceutical research, encouraging young scientists, and fostering scientific exchange. Through symposia, awards, and collaborative initiatives, SPSF has been at the forefront of promoting knowledge that drives innovation and improves patient care.

About The Symposium

Autoimmune diseases affect millions globally, leading to chronic health conditions with significant personal, social, and economic impacts. This symposium will highlight the latest advancements and foster collaboration.

Significance & Focus Areas

  • Chronic Conditions: Impact and potential for improved treatments.
  • Immune System Insights: Relevance to broader medical applications.
  • Genetics & Environment: Interplay in autoimmune diseases.
  • Targeted Therapies: Latest advancements.
  • Public Health Impact: Economic and societal implications.
  • Scientific Breakthroughs: Cross-disciplinary advancements.
